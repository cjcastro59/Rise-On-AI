Immediately accessible crisis support hub. Triggered when AI detects high-distress language, or manually via the always-visible emergency button. Warm, non-clinical, and action-oriented.

Page 11 / 11
Design Notes
  • Accessible without login — the emergency pill in navbar always links here
  • Warm, human tone — avoids clinical coldness; uses empathetic language
  • Crisis hotline cards with click-to-call functionality
  • AI detects distress patterns and proactively surfaces this page with a gentle banner
  • Breathing/grounding exercise widget available immediately for in-the-moment relief
  • University counselor contact card at the top for localized support
